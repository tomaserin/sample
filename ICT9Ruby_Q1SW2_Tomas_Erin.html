5 Characters from Noli Me Tangere

5 Characters from Noli Me Tangere
Elias
Elias was a fugitive who resented the power of the Catholic Church and Spanish government over the Philippines.
Maria Clara
Maria Clara is one of the main characters in Noli and the love interest for Ibarra.
Donya Victorina
She is a Filipino who'd rather be a Spaniard or mestiza than be her real ethnicity, Filipino.
Padre Damaso
Padre Damaso is described as an arrogant or self-centered person. Because he was a friar, he abused his power and acted more superior towards others. He did not like natives who studied abroad.
Juan Crisastomo Ibarra y Magsalin
Ibarra is a mestiza who studied in Europe for 7 ears. He is also the main character of the book. He's described as a wealthy, idealistic man. He wanted to improve the country and believed the power of education. Ibarra is also believed to be a persona or self-insert of Jose Rizal himself.
